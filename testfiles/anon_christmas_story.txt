It was the day after Christmas, and the snow lay thick upon the ground, like a great white blanket covering the earth. The air was crisp and cold, and the trees stood bare against the winter sky. In the little village, children rejoiced as they played in the freshly fallen snow, their laughter filling the air. It was a time of joy and merriment, a time for families to come together and share in the warmth of the season. As the evening drew near, the villagers gathered in the town square, their faces glowing with excitement. The annual Christmas feast was about to begin, and the aroma of roasted turkey and freshly baked pies wafted through the air
